Learn more about Bookamania

Bookamania Ltd is a registered and well established book company located in Lagos, Nigeria.

We have been in operation since 2010. Our stores have a wide variety of books from global publishers. We have also been able to get the exclusive distribution rights in Nigeria for some publishers overseas. These publishers refer their prospective customers in Nigeria to us.

COMPETITIVE PRICING

Our prices are highly competitive and reasonable. We offer you great value for every transaction with us.

PIRACY

At Bookamia, we recognize the creativity and immense resources that go into book publishing, as such, we do not stock pirated materials in our stores.

WHOLESALE AND RETAIL

Aside our wholesale sales to institutional customers, we also attend to the retail needs of our individual customers.

PROMPT AND EXCEPTIONAL SERVICE DELIVERY

At Bookamania we know that your time is precious. As such, we do not delay in delivering your orders and services. We keep our customers and clients informed when situations beyond our control arise. For instance, when a book you order is out of print or temporarily unavailable from the publisher.

CUSTOMER BASE

We pride at having a long standing and prestigious customer base. This is because we service reputable schools and organizations over the years.

BOOK FAIRS AND ARTS FESTIVALS

We attend book fairs and exhibitions around the globe. We use these events to establish contacts, familiarize with industry players, share information and stay on the know on current publications and evolving issues in the industry. We also visit major bookstores in several countries in order to be innovative and stay ahead in this competitive market. As well as to serve you better.

LIBRARY SERVICES

We also offer library development, stocking and general services for schools and organizations, as well as individuals.

PUBLISHING

Due to popular demand by schools using the Nigerian curriculum, we have also gone into research with reputable authors and educationists to publish books with rich blend of the Nigerian curriculum.

Our publishing division publishes books under the imprint of Bp. We publish genres such as: educational curriculum-based textbooks, autobiographies, biographies, fiction, nonfiction, children literature and poetry. We also accept and consider manuscripts from authors for publication.
